Which category is for
my child?

At Al Hakim, we offer a tailored programme best for their ages.
Each category has activities that focus on how to make them
always learn and grow in the best way.

Little Owl

3 to 12 months

Focus on sensory stimulation, bonding, and motor skills.

Activities:

  • Tummy time
  • Baby gym
  • Massage
  • Soft toys play

Tiny Tots

1 year

Hands-on learning through play, basic cognitive skills, social interaction.

Activities:

  • Creative play
  • Basic counting
  • Storytelling
  • Role play

Awesome Kids

2 years

Encouraging first steps, communication, and basic independence.

Activities:

  • Flashcards
  • Sensory play
  • Music & movement

Amazing Kids

3 & 4 years

Preparing for structured learning, emotional development, and creativity.

Activities:

  • Early literacy
  • Teamwork
  • Theme-based learning
  • Exploration
