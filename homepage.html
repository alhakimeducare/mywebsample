Vision

Creating a conductable environment to enhance children's early development holistically includes six GEM early development areas: physical, intellect, language, emotional, social development, and morals.

Mission

Providing quality learning independently through play in parallel with the national curriculum concept, in addition to providing an interactive, clean, and safe environment for children. We also provide trained and qualified educators and apply pure values in child care
